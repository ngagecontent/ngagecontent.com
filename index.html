MARKETING DOESN’T HAVE TO BE HARD

Marketing seems tough in the digital age. You know you need to put an emphasis on driving qualified leads to your sales team. But how can you be an expert on the channels to use, let alone what messages you create? That’s where NgageContent comes in. Our performance-driven blueprint gives you the strategies, resources and technology to build and measure marketing efforts customized for you.

CONNECTING CONTENT TO YOUR SALES FUNNEL

Bottom line results come from an engaged sales funnel. Done correctly, marketing blends into your sales process to help you close more leads faster. At NgageContent, we do the research work to get a full understanding of your buyer audience and then create content that takes them from their first interaction with you all the way through their buyer journey, enhancing and complementing your existing outbound sales process.

ENGAGE, EDUCATE AND CONVERT

We study your audience and your market opportunities so we can devise content strategies that take a full-funnel approach to marketing, building awareness at the top of the funnel, generating interested leads and helping them make a decision in the middle, and increasing conversions at the bottom. We focus on telling them stories that solve their problem and pointing them toward your solutions.

Awareness

We attract leads to your site and answer common industry questions with compelling content, helping identify you as an industry expert.

Consideration

As we work your leads through their buying options, we arm them with content targeted toward making a purchase decision.

Decision

As your lead starts to make a decision, we show them how you solve problems for them and what the buying process looks like.

Conversion

Now qualified, your prospect gets regular content that engages them with how to purchase from you and what happens after they buy.

BOTTOM LINE RESULTS

By understanding the behaviors, triggers and pain points of your audience, we drive better leads at a lower cost. Our blueprint helps you see clearly measured analytics tied to the campaigns we create. This is how you make modern marketing easy and have the results to show for it.
